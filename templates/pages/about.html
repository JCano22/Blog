About!

The purpose of this blog is to help the beginner who doesn’t want to be a beginner anymore. If all you do is learn songs from guitar tabs or YouTube you’ll have a hard time learning to fly. The self-taught guitarist will eventually run out of runway.
This blog is designed to help the longtime beginner do more than just memorize songs from tabs and videos. If you’ve already played guitar for a while and still haven’t made much progress, this could be the right course for you.
